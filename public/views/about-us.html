About Us

Who We Are

At Printique, we believe your home should reflect you. That’s why we merge creativity with 3D printing technology — allowing you to design décor that’s as unique as your personality. Our platform lets you customize lamps, shelves, tables, and more using your own ideas or AI-generated designs.

Our Vision

To revolutionize home décor by making personalization, creativity, and sustainability accessible to everyone — transforming houses into spaces that truly feel like home.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to design and own one-of-a-kind 3D-printed décor by:

  • Offering easy-to-use online customization tools and AI design support.
  • Using sustainable and affordable 3D printing materials.
  • Providing Augmented Reality previews before printing.
  • Delivering eco-friendly products that blend art, technology, and individuality.
